Software Engineering PhD Candidate @ UCL


Hi, I'm Dave and I'm a second-year PhD candidate at University College London (UCL). My research is primarily focused on understanding the software developer experience in an Automated Software Engineering (ASE) landscape. I am working as a member of both the SOLAR and CREST research groups.

You can contact me at david.williams.22@ucl.ac.uk.
